# 🎭 Voice of Customer Analytics Pipeline  
### Scalable Review Intelligence for Experience Benchmarking

This project builds a **data pipeline for extracting and analyzing large-scale user-generated reviews** to uncover patterns in visitor experience across venues.

Using Google Reviews as a data source, the system transforms unstructured text into **structured insights on sentiment, topics, and pain points**, enabling comparison across locations and identifying high-impact areas for improvement.

---

## 🚀 What This Actually Does

- Scrapes and structures **review-level data** (text, rating, metadata)
- Performs **sentiment analysis and topic extraction**
- Identifies **high-frequency themes and pain points**
- Tracks **experience trends over time**
- Enables **cross-venue benchmarking**

👉 The output is a **decision-ready dataset + insight layer**, not just raw text

---

## 🧠 Why It Matters

Most organizations rely on anecdotal feedback or small samples.

This system:
- Captures **real user voice at scale**
- Identifies **systemic experience issues (e.g., parking, seating, navigation)**
- Enables **data-driven prioritization across multiple locations**
- Provides a foundation for **continuous experience monitoring**

---

## ⚙️ Technical Approach

- **Data Collection:** Automated scraping of Google Reviews (text, rating, timestamp, metadata)  
- **Data Processing:** Cleaning and structuring using Python (Pandas)  
- **Text Analysis:**  
  - Keyword frequency analysis  
  - Bigram / phrase extraction  
  - Topic grouping (UX vs spatial/architectural)  
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Classification of positive, neutral, and negative feedback  
- **Trend Analysis:** Time-series tracking of sentiment and issues  
- **Visualization:** Matplotlib / Seaborn for insight communication  

---

## 📊 Scale of Analysis

- Multiple venues analyzed across a network  
- Hundreds of reviews per venue (100+ per location)  
- High-quality reviewers prioritized (e.g., local guides)  
- Multi-year time horizon for trend detection  

---

## 📈 Example Insights

- **Parking and seating** consistently emerge as dominant experience drivers  
- **Operational friction (staff, navigation)** appears in clustered time periods  
- Positive sentiment is high overall, but **specific pain points disproportionately shape perception**

These patterns allow teams to:
- Target **high-impact improvements**
- Benchmark performance across venues
- Track how experience evolves over time

---

## 🧩 Use Cases

- Experience benchmarking across locations  
- Customer journey optimization  
- Identifying operational vs spatial issues  
- Supporting design and service improvements  
- Building voice-of-customer dashboards
